callAR

Customer Service through AR

callAR is an implementation of customer service through AR for the T-Mobile 5G Hackathon. During an era of covid, maintaining safety with no compromise to customer service is essential. AR is a powerful tool that gives us the ability to have human interaction without the need of masks or social distancing. Even after covid has been controlled, many may not have the ability to travel to a store to receive in-person customer service. With callAR, the disparity can be lessened and additional adaptationscan be made to accomodate anybody regardless of socioeconomic status.
